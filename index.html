What is FaMealia?

The FaMealia is not your average picnic lunch box. It stores solar energy in the form of a tiny panel included in the cover of the box. It includes a solar-powered lunch, meal heater and meal cooler; it contains a button where you can set the temperature. It also contains charger ports; a solar-powered power bank that can charge your smartphone which is a daily necessity; it doesn’t need to be charged via any electrical devices in order to work. When not in use, the actual portion that will store the food or meal can be removed for cleaning. The enclosure outside of the box is made of heat-resistant silicone in order for it to retract or expand when the meal is empty and not in use anymore.

The FaMealia Vision

FaMealia offers a solar-powered lunch box that enables families to preserve food, protect the environment, and create lasting memories. Our vision is to be one of the most reliable and flexible lunch boxes in the industry – by making superior quality and unmatched service.
